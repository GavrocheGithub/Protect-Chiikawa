# Protect-Chiikawa
Interactive Media end of semester assignment

Click this link to play the game: https://gavrochegithub.github.io/Protect-Chiikawa/

# Artistic Concept
The inspiration of this project is from the explosive popularity of the Chiikawa (ちいかわ) IP and its massive advertisement in Hong Kong. After the magnificent Chiikawa exhibition at K11, East Tsim Sha Tsui last August (https://www.discoverhongkong.com/eng/themed-travel/chiikawa) and the pervasive advertisements covering MTR trains, I was captivated by the adorable appearance of these characters. Chiikawa characters are deeply loved for their innocent, fragile, and often anxious dispositions except Usagi. By placing them in a harsh and severe environment and giving players an umbrella to protect them, the game evokes a strong sense of active guardianship.

# Relevant Exsisting Work
Traditional arcade dodging games, such as Flappy Bird (https://flappybird.io/)  typically require players to navigate an avatar directly through environmental hazards. This mini game also subverts this mechanic.
